So how do I structure my workouts?

Going off the checklist and other factors, the best splits are Upper Lower and Full Body

I am going to give you templates for both and some pointers for both, edit the workouts how you want, do what you want.
Full Body Tips: The goal is to hit a muscle at least 2-3 times a week, some muscles can be hit 3 times a week and some might be not able to. Muscles that are easily damaged include the chest, biceps, triceps, and hamstrings. If you can recover fast enough to hit these muscles 3x a week go right ahead.
Upper Lower Tips: The goal here is to hit a muscle 2 times a week and recover the best, which is why it's better than PPL(Push Pull Legs).
Add-remove volume, replace exercises as you like.
