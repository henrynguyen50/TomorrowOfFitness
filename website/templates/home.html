Basic Pointers

Bulking is not needed to build "more" muscle
Muscle is not built through microtears
Zero intensifiers (drop sets) (finishers) are needed
Time under tension is a useless metric
The "stretch" or maximum range of motion is not needed
Adding or subtracting volume during a bulk or cut is useless

The Checklist

Train 3-4 Days a week (FB or UL)
Hit each muscle 2-3 times a week
Lift in the range of 4-8 reps
Increase weight after reaching 8+ repetitions to Progressively Overload
Train to near failure, leave 1-2 repetitions in the tank
Rest 2+ minutes between sets and exercises
Have a good sleep schedule, 7+ hours
